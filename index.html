Hey, my name is Vitali Altuchow. I am 29 years old and live in Aachen, a beautiful city in Germany. And I am a passionate programmer! That's why I started to study computer science after my job as a mechatronic. It was a good start which I am grateful for. During a student job I discovered my passion for frontend development.
